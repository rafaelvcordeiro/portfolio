Mobile Robot


Description
We created simple and effective products focused on confort and wellbeing during pandemics to aid better working from home.
All products were carefully made in my factory Fábrica Jangada, a respectful manufacturing business, using only sustainable sourced plywood.
Year: 2011
Team: Dr. José Tarcísio Costa, Dr. Marcus Vinícius Veloso and Rafael Cordeiro.
My role: was responsible for mechanical parts design, electronic circuits and PCB design, sensors and manufacturing of the robot.
Key Achievements:
• Designed a reliable and low cost USB Data Acquisition System (DAS), using native USB protocol with PIC18F4550 microcontroller, reducing costs by 90%
• Patent deposit request at INPI ( PI 1106417-0 A2)
