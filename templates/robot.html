Mobile Robot


Description
Autonomous mobile robot with wheels for surveillance of environments and research activities, capable of sending images in real time and being controlled via the internet.
Year: 2011
Team: Dr. José Tarcísio Costa, Dr. Marcus Vinícius Veloso and Rafael Cordeiro.
Robot System: Dr. Marcus Vinícius Veloso developed SOM4R, a Middleware for Robotic Applications Based on the Resource-Oriented Architecture.
My role: I was responsible for mechanical parts design, electronic circuits and PCB design, sensors and manufacturing of the robot.
Key Achievements:
• Designed a reliable and low cost USB Data Acquisition System (DAS), using native USB protocol with PIC18F4550 microcontroller, reducing costs by 90%
• Patent deposit request at INPI ( PI 1106417-0 A2)
